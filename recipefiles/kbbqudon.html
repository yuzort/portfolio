Ingredients


• egg
• green onions
• red peppers
• green peppers
• garlic
• ginger
• carrots
• udon noodles
• steak
• Korean spice rub
• Korean bbq sauce
• soy sauce
• teriyaki

Instructions


1. Combine Korean spice rub, Korean bbq sauce, soy sauce, and teriyaki sauce for the rub.
2. Marinate the steak in the rub.
3. COOK MEAT
4. BOIL NOODLES
5. Stir fry egg, green onions, red peppers, green peppers, garlic, ginger, and carrots.
6. Serve with steak over the udon noodles.
7. Sprinkle chopped green onions on top for garnish.
