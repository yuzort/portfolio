Hello, I’m Yuzuka! I’ve developed my copywriting expertise through 3 years of content marketing and 5 years of coaching + editing writing for C-suite leaders, high school students, journalists, local government staff, peers, and more. As the 48th hire and first writing-focused team member at a startup that has since doubled in size and been acquired, I’m skilled at collaborating in a high-growth team and SaaS company.

I've worked on issues in climate tech/sustainability, cybersecurity, higher education access, public finance, and regional economic development. I also have experience in project management, operations, web design, and writing coaching. Some of my wins include:
