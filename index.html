Hello!

Hi, I'm Yuzuka! With my marketing and communications background, I've worked with C-suite leaders, high school students, journalists, local political campaigns, and more to support their storytelling. I'm always thinking, "How can I make information easier to understand?"

I've worked on issues in energy efficiency/sustainability, cybersecurity, higher education access, public finance, and regional economic development. I also have experience in project management, operations, web design, and writing coaching. Some of my wins include:

  • filling in (with a day's notice) to translate Japanese in cybersecurity engineering meetings,
  • contributing technical writing that supported my company’s entry into the U.S. Department of Energy’s National Renewable Energy Lab’s (NREL) clean energy management incubator,
  • implementing a marketing platform that improved a process's efficiency by over 75%,
  • and creating a document explaining credit freezes to help friends take action to become more secure against credit card fraud

Outside of work, I’m aspiring to be better at rollerskating and embroidering. I love to read and I'm currently reading There There by Tommy Orange and No Planet B edited by Teen Vogue.
