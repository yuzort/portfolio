John Bucy III for Texas

2022

Challenge

Representative John Bucy III (Texas House of Representatives, District 136) was running a highly contested re-election campaign. Through Tech for Campaigns, I volunteered on a team to design Representative Bucy's re-election campaign and constituent services website.

Skills

  • Copywriting
  • Policy research
  • Design support

Solution

I revised existing copy and wrote a majority of the copy on the site, fact-checked and referenced his policies, and collaborated with the designer to best inform and engage his constituents. Representative Bucy won his re-election campaign and his last election! Check out the website here.
