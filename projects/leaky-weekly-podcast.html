Leaky Weekly Podcast

Flare Research (2024–Present)

The Challenge

We had attempted several podcast and video projects previously, but lacked the bandwidth and established processes to sustain them. To build a "cult brand" identity and gain a competitive edge in the cybersecurity video space, I sought to formalize our production and strategy.

Skills

  • Cybercrime Research & Scriptwriting
  • End-to-End Project Management
  • Cross-functional Collaboration (Design/Audio/Video)

The Solution

I recruited a podcast consultant and editing expert to bridge the gap from idea to reality. Today, Leaky Weekly is the premier video podcast in the Threat Exposure Management space. Our updated strategy led to a 30% increase in YouTube Shorts views, and by optimizing the workflow, I reduced the host’s workload by 90%.
