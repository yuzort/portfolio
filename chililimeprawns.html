Chili Lime Prawns on Paprika Rice

Ingredients


• prawns
• cumin
• paprika
• chili powder
• lime juice
• garlic
• cauliflower rice
• spinach

Instructions


1. Combine cumin, paprika, chili powder, lime juice, and garlic to make the rub.
2. Marinate the prawns in the rub.
3. Sear prawns on oiled skillet for 3-5 mins until shells are crispy, flip and cook for 2 minutes.
4. Set cooked prawns aside, then sauté spinach in same skillet.
5. Cook cauliflower rice. Mix spinach into rice when done.
6. Serve with prawns over the rice.
